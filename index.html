BlackLivesMatter

ABOUT

The movement, BlackLivesMatter, was founded in 2013 after the death of a black teenage boy, TrayVon Martin, who was fatally shot by a Hispanic American. Ever since then, Blacks have been protesting all over the US to stop police brutality and racisim towards Black Americans.
